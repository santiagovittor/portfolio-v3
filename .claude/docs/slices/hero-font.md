# Hero Section Typography Specification: Hand-Painted Mid-Century Noir Door Lettering

## Objective
Implement a highly artistic, premium typography style for the website's hero section header. The goal is to recreate the precise aesthetic of a hand-painted 1940s–1950s professional office door (e.g., an attorney, private investigator, or vintage agency) as seen in classic glass gilding and sign painting. It must look like authentic, hand-crafted art rather than a sterile digital font.

---

## 1. Typographic Style & Anatomy
* **Genre:** Mid-Century Art Deco / Vintage Noir Display Lettering (referencing the "Wood & Wood Detective Agency" door style).
* **Anatomy & Geometry:** * Exaggerated, high-waisted geometric crossbars on characters like **A**, **E**, and **H**.
    * Sharp, stylized geometric angles and elongated tails (e.g., a high-reaching, stylized **Y**).
    * Distinctive concave or split-serif points on terminal edges (visible on characters like **W** and **D**).
* **Dimensional Layering:**
    * **Core Fill:** A solid, thick inner body (e.g., crisp white or vintage cream).
    * **Contour Line:** A clean, prominent outer stroke framing the entire letterform to provide stark contrast.
    * **Cast Shadow:** A sharp, hard-edged, solid 3D drop shadow offset to the bottom-right, giving the illusion that the painted text is floating on a glass surface.

---

## 2. Hand-Painted Artistic Execution
* **Texture & Viscosity:** The final render must look like it was physically painted with a sign-writer's lettering brush using heavy enamel paint.
* **Organic Imperfections:** It should feature subtle brush stroke textures, organic edges, microscopic paint bleeding, or hand-painted weight variations. It must not look like a mathematically perfect vector or generic digital font.
* **Artistic Depth:** The text needs to convey the organic feel of a real human hand controlling the paint brush strokes.

---

## 3. Entrance & Animation Behavior
* **Tone:** Graceful, smooth, and deeply elegant. It must seamlessly match a premium, high-end hero section without being flashy, gamified, or jarring.
* **Style:** The reveal should feel fluid and artistic—such as a progressive, graceful stroke-by-stroke materialization, a smooth canvas-like wipe, or an elegant fade that naturally reveals the organic paint textures as it settles into place.
