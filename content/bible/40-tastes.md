## Music

Abbey Road and Making Movies are the desert-island records. The Beatles
for the songwriting, Dire Straits for the guitar tone I'll never get out
of my head. Music is usually on while I work. Joaquín Sabina, Charly Garcia, Andres Calamaro, Gustavo Cerati.
I was a diehard Oasis fan when i was younger specially for albums until The Masterplan and will always have a soft spot for them. I also love 80s synthpop music like Depeche Mode and Tears for Fears. I have my 90s hip hop taste with 2pac, Notorious B.I.G and A tribe called quest. Right now I am listening to a lot of Leonard Cohen, Closing time being my go-track this days.

## Cooking

Italian, mostly — it's the cooking I grew up around in Argentina. Pasta
from scratch when there's time. Pizza as well. I love oven food that takes time.

## Sports

I follow sports the Argentine way: seriously. Lifetime River Plate fan, I've been a Lio Messi fan since I was a kid and never doubted him for a second, not only because of stats but because he is clearly a pleasure to watch if you love the sport. I also love basketball and have been following the Golden State Warriors since 2015, when Steph Curry emerged as the best shooter ever and one of the best players to ever play. Being a 90s kid, though, Michael Jordan is the GOAT for me.
